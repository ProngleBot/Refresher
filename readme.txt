Don't have a keyboard at hand? But you want to refresh a PWA?
This may sound like a specific scenario but its actually quite common, 
Fear not Refresher is here just for that. With one swipe of a mouse you can now 
refresh whatever you wanted to refresh. 

Instructions on how to operate the Refresher:
1) Open refresher.exe.
2) Move your mouse over to the top left side of your screen.
3) Close the terminal/command prompt window to end Refresher.
